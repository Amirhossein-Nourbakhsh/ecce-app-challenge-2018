Extend your Roofs Life & Increase Property Value

Green roofs last longer than conventional roofs as a result of protection given by the substrate and vegetation, eliminating thermos stresses by reducing the exposure of roofing material to extreme temperatures and ultraviolet light. Green roofs also eliminate the pooling of water, decrease the total volume of storm water run off and improve the fluidity of the remaining run off. It is estimated that installing a green roof should extent the life of the roof 2-3 times of a conventional roof lifespan.
